# QFC Network — Team

## Naming Convention

Every team member must have a Chinese name（中文名）listed alongside their English name.

**Signature format for issues, PRs, and official communication:**

> 🤖/👤 *[Action] by [English Name]（[中文名]）, [Role] @ QFC Network — via [Tool/Platform]*

- 👤 for humans
- 🤖 for AI agents

Examples:
- 👤 *Approved by Larry Lai（来拉里）, Founder @ QFC Network*
- 🤖 *Filed by Jarvis Lam（林哲维）, QA Engineer @ QFC Network — via OpenClaw*
